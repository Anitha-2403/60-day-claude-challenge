The E85 Paradox

⚠️ High Cost, Lowest Emissions

E85 (Flex-Fuel) is the most expensive fuel per km (₹6.37) — pricier even than plain Petrol — yet it produces the lowest CO₂ per km (0.070 kg) of every fuel type in the dataset. Its maintenance cost (₹0.460/km) stays on par with Petrol, not worse.

Break-even check: E85 price would need to fall to ~₹79.2/L (from ₹82/L) to match Petrol's running cost — a small ~3.4% price gap standing between E85 and cost-parity.

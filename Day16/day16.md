# Day 16 – Claude Custom Skill: Stock Fundamental Research

## 🎯 Objective

The objective of this task was to create a reusable Custom Skill in Claude that analyzes Indian and global listed companies using fundamental analysis. The skill generates structured, evidence-based stock research reports while avoiding investment recommendations.

---

## 🛠️ Custom Skill Details

**Skill Name:**
stock-fundamental-research

**Description:**
Analyze Indian and global listed companies using fundamentals, financial statements, business quality, competitive advantages, valuation, risks, and growth prospects. Generate evidence-based research reports and investor-friendly summaries. Never provide direct buy, sell, or hold recommendations.

---

## 📊 Stock Analyzed

**Company:** Tata Consultancy Services (TCS)

**Analysis Mode:** Deep Dive Fundamental Research

---

## 📌 Report Highlights

The generated report included:

- Company Overview
- Current Market Price (CMP)
- Market Capitalization
- 52-Week High & Low
- Valuation Analysis (P/E, P/B, EV/EBITDA)
- Revenue & Profit Growth
- Financial Health Analysis
- ROE & ROCE
- Debt Analysis
- Dividend History
- Promoter, FII & DII Holdings
- Peer Comparison
- Business Strengths
- Risk Factors
- Overall Fundamental Quality
- Educational Disclaimer

---

## 📚 Key Learnings

- Learned how to create reusable Custom Skills in Claude.
- Understood how Custom Skills eliminate the need to paste long prompts repeatedly.
- Explored AI-assisted fundamental stock research.
- Learned to interpret company financial metrics and valuation data.
- Understood the importance of evidence-based analysis and avoiding unsupported investment recommendations.

---

## 💡 Biggest Takeaway

Creating a Custom Skill makes repetitive workflows much faster and more consistent. Instead of rewriting prompts each time, the saved skill can be reused to generate detailed stock research reports for different companies with a single request.

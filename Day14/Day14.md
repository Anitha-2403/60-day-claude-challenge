# Day 14 – AI Job Red Flag Detector

## 🎯 Objective

The objective of this task was to use AI to evaluate a job opportunity by analyzing its job description and company information. The goal was to identify potential red flags, assess hiring risks, understand company credibility, and make an informed decision before applying.

---

## 📌 Job Details

**Company:** PLACEMEIN

**Role:** Data Analyst Intern

**Location:** Hyderabad, Telangana, India

**Work Mode:** On-site

**Salary:** ₹18,000 – ₹20,000 per month

---

## 🏢 Company Information

* **Industry:** Recruitment and Talent Acquisition
* **Company Type:** Recruitment & HR Services
* **Location:** Hyderabad, Telangana
* **Work Model:** On-site Internship

**Overview:**

PLACEMEIN is a recruitment and talent acquisition company that helps students and fresh graduates connect with internship and employment opportunities across multiple industries.

---

## 🤖 AI Risk Analysis

Using Claude AI, I analyzed the job description and company information to identify hiring risks, workplace signals, and company transparency.

### 📊 Overall Risk Score

**52 / 100 – Moderate Risk**

---

## 🚩 Top Red Flags

* Recruitment agency with no end-client information disclosed.
* Internship duration not specified.
* No mention of a Pre-Placement Offer (PPO) or full-time conversion.
* Limited information about company size and client portfolio.
* Generic job description with minimal role-specific details.

---

## ✅ Positive Signals

* Salary range clearly disclosed.
* Internship requirements are realistic for freshers.
* Responsibilities are clearly defined.
* Broad eligibility criteria.
* On-site work model honestly mentioned.
* No unrealistic expectations or toxic workplace language.

---

## ⚖️ Final Verdict

**⚠️ Apply with Caution**

The internship appears legitimate and suitable for freshers. However, applicants should verify important details such as the client company, internship duration, reporting structure, and PPO opportunities before accepting an offer.

---

## 💬 Smart Interview Questions

1. Is this internship for PLACEMEIN or one of its client companies?
2. What is the exact duration of the internship?
3. Is there a PPO or full-time conversion opportunity after successful completion?
4. What projects and technologies will interns work on?
5. Are there any training fees, deposits, or additional charges during onboarding?

---

## 📚 Key Learnings

* AI can quickly identify hidden risks within job descriptions.
* Company transparency is an important factor before applying.
* Salary disclosure and realistic job expectations are positive indicators.
* Asking relevant interview questions helps candidates make informed career decisions.
* Researching companies before applying reduces the chances of joining unsuitable organizations.

---

## 💡 Biggest Insight

A job description may appear attractive at first glance, but carefully analyzing hiring practices, company transparency, and role expectations can reveal important details. AI-powered job analysis helps job seekers make smarter and safer career decisions.

---

## ✅ Conclusion

This activity demonstrated how AI can support job seekers by evaluating opportunities, identifying potential risks, highlighting positive signals, and recommending meaningful interview questions. It emphasized the importance of researching employers and making informed decisions before applying for internships or full-time roles.

# Day 19 – Football Intelligence Hub using Claude AI

## 🎯 Objective

The objective of this task was to use Claude AI to analyze a football workbook and generate an interactive Football Intelligence Experience. The activity included World Cup predictions, a Football IQ assessment, a personality analysis inspired by football legends, and a final personalized Football Intelligence Profile.
